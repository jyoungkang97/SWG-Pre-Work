Californication

Red Hot Chili Peppers

June 8, 1999

A reunited Red Hot Chili Peppers back with their first album in four years, in what may be their most anticipated album ever with over 15 million copies sold worldwide.

Band Members

Anthony Kiedis - lead vocals

Flea - bass, backing vocals

John Frusciante - guitars, backing vocals, keyboard

Chad Smith - drums
