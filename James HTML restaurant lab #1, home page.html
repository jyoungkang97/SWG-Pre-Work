J.K. Rolls specializes in gourmet Japanese and Korean style sushi rolls, using ingredients found from around the world. Our Scottish Salmon is of the highest grade, and our Blue Fin tuna is caught straight from Alaskan waters. Established in 1983, J.K. Rolls is a family restaurant, where our mission is to bring authentic, high quality rolls to Minneapolis, without breaking your bank account.
